Mission Statement

Why This Project Exists

The Hallucination Identification turns AI literacy into a library game. Each room asks you to read a short passage and question every sentence by separating polished misinformation from grounded truth.

The aim is to practice the habit of slowing down and gaining confidence in what is the truth and what is false; a hallucination.

Each level shifts subjects and tone so you can test the same critical-thinking skill across science, history, literature, and beyond.

How To Play

Step 1

Generate a fresh passage for the level you choose.

Step 2

Click the sentences you believe are hallucinations.

Step 3

Submit your verdict and compare your read to the truth notes.

Keep In Mind

Strong wording, technical vocabulary, and confident delivery can all make a false statement feel trustworthy. The challenge is designed to make you notice that instinct and test it.
